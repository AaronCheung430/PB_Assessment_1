iPhone 14 Pro now rumored to feature ‘hole + pill design’ instead of notch
Rumors
12.01.2022

Initially, it was reported that Apple would adopt a hole-punch design to house the camera, but now reliable analyst Ross Young says that Apple will use a “hole + pill design” on the iPhone 14 Pro and iPhone 14 Pro Max.
